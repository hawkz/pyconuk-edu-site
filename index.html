What is it?

PyconUK is the UK's premier community organised conference about the Python programming language.

On Friday 19th September teachers and developers will come together to collaborate on practical pythonic pedagogy. On Saturday 20th September we will welcome kids (young and old) for inspiring adventures in code.

The PyconUK education track is all about the wonderful things that happen when diverse groups of people come together to work with fun technology. We'd like to thank our sponsors, without whom this event could not take place: Bank of America, The Python Software Foundation, The Raspberry Pi Foundation and our very own PyconUK Society.

For Teachers

Learn to use Python in your classroom!

  • Learn Python with experienced teachers and expert developers.
  • Collaborate with programmers to create and share educational resources.
  • Network with teaching colleagues and contacts in industry.
  • Have fun exploring technology that will inspire your students.

The teacher's professional development day is on Friday 19th September in Coventry. The education team from the RaspberryPi Foundation, fellow teachers and an international group of Python experts will be on hand to help you get the most out of the day.

But what about the cost..?

We have you covered! (Literally)

Thanks to the very generous sponsorship of Bank of America we will contribute £200 to your school for the cost of a cover teacher. Furthermore, your ticket for the whole four day conference costs only £47 (compared to £150 for a regular full conference ticket).

But space is limited! The contribution for a cover teacher is limited to the first twenty teachers who book a ticket.

Book now to avoid disappointment!

For Kids

Do you like robots? Ever fancied programming Minecraft? Want to learn cool technical tricks from expert programmers? How about taking part in coding workshops?

Yes?

Great!

You (or your kids) should come along to the PyconUK kid's day on Saturday 20th September in Coventry (9:30am - 3:30pm). Kid's tickets cost just £5 each. All kids must be 18 years old or younger and attend with an adult who must remain on site at all times (accompanying adults get in for free). We'll even throw in a baked potato for lunch!

Book now, because places are limited!

Glad you asked! Mums and sons, dads and daughters and developers and teachers come together for exciting adventures in code. Book Now

Bookings can be made through the form on our registration page:

https://pyconuk2014.eventbrite.co.uk

Instructions for booking tickets for teachers and kids are as follows:

Teachers

  1. Click the "Enter promotional code" link just above the big green "Order Now" button.
  2. In the box that appears type the promotional code: MORTAR_BOARD then click the "Apply" button.
  3. The "Regular Teacher" option will appear on the form, select the number of tickets you require for this option and click the "Order Now" button.
  4. Fill in your details and be prepared to pay for your tickets.
  5. When you attend PyconUK on the 19th September you will receive details for your school bursar so they can invoice PyconUK for the £200 contribution for a cover teacher.

Kids

  1. Click the "Enter promotional code" link just above the big green "Order Now" button.
  2. In the box that appears type the promotional code: DISCIPULUM then click the "Apply" button.
  3. The "Young Person" option will appear on the form, select the number of tickets you require for this option and click the "Order Now" button.
  4. Fill in the details for each attendee and be prepared to pay for your tickets.

If you have any questions or encounter a problem with your booking please don't hesitate to contact us via the "Contact PyCon UK Society" link on the registration page.
